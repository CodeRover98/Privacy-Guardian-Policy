Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 6, 2025

Introduction

Privacy Guardian ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Android application collects, uses, and safeguards your information.

Our Core Principle: Your privacy data stays on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or share any of your personal information with third parties.

Information We Collect

Privacy Guardian analyzes apps installed on your device to help you understand potential privacy risks. The app collects the following information locally on your device only:

1. Installed Applications List

  • What: Names and package identifiers of apps installed on your device
  • Why: To scan and analyze which apps you have installed
  • How: Using Android's QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES permission
  • Storage: Stored locally in the app's database on your device

2. App Permissions Data

  • What: List of permissions requested by each installed app
  • Why: To calculate privacy risk scores and identify potentially invasive apps
  • How: Analyzing each app's manifest through Android PackageManager
  • Storage: Stored locally in the app's database on your device

3. Network Usage Data (Optional)

  • What: Data consumption statistics per app (WiFi and mobile data)
  • Why: To help you monitor which apps use your data
  • How: Using Android's UsageStatsManager (requires user permission)
  • Storage: Stored locally in the app's database on your device

4. App Settings and Preferences

  • What: Your app configuration (scan intervals, notification preferences)
  • Why: To remember your preferences and settings
  • How: Stored in Android SharedPreferences
  • Storage: Stored locally on your device

How We Use Your Information

All information collected by Privacy Guardian is used exclusively on your device for the following purposes:

  • Privacy Score Calculation: Analyzing app permissions to calculate privacy risk scores
  • App Monitoring: Tracking which apps access sensitive permissions
  • Data Usage Tracking: Showing you how much data each app consumes
  • Privacy Alerts: Notifying you about apps with high privacy risks
  • User Experience: Remembering your settings and preferences

Information Sharing and Third Parties

We do NOT:

  • Collect any data from your device to our servers
  • Share your information with third parties
  • Sell your data to advertisers
  • Use analytics or tracking services
  • Transmit any data over the internet

All data stays on your device. Privacy Guardian operates entirely offline and does not require an internet connection.

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage Only

  • All data is stored in a local database on your device using Android Room Database
  • No cloud synchronization or backup (unless you manually use Android's built-in backup)
  • Data is protected by Android's app sandboxing security model

Data Protection

  • Your data is accessible only by Privacy Guardian
  • Other apps cannot access Privacy Guardian's database
  • Data is stored in your device's internal storage (protected by Android OS)

Your Data Rights

Access Your Data

You can view all collected data within the app:

  • Dashboard shows summary statistics
  • Apps List shows all scanned apps and their scores
  • App Details shows permissions for each app

Delete Your Data

You have full control to delete all data at any time:

  1. Open Privacy Guardian
  2. Navigate to Settings
  3. Tap "Clear All Data"
  4. Confirm deletion

This will permanently delete all scanned app information, app privacy scores, usage statistics, and app settings.

Uninstalling the App

Uninstalling Privacy Guardian automatically removes all data from your device, as per Android's standard app behavior.

Permissions We Request

1. QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES (Required)

  • Purpose: To access the list of installed apps on your device
  • Disclosure: You will be shown a prominent disclosure dialog explaining this before first use
  • Required: Yes - the app cannot function without this permission

2. PACKAGE_USAGE_STATS (Optional)

  • Purpose: To monitor app usage statistics and data consumption
  • How to Grant: Settings > Enable Monitoring toggle
  • Required: No - app works without it, but with limited functionality

3. POST_NOTIFICATIONS (Optional - Android 13+)

  • Purpose: To send you privacy alerts about high-risk apps
  • Required: No - you can disable notifications

Children's Privacy

Privacy Guardian does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences and does not target children. Parents should supervise their children's use of mobile devices.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes:

  • We will update the "Last Updated" date at the top
  • Significant changes will be communicated through an in-app notification
  • Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy

You can always view the latest version of this policy in the app: Settings > Privacy Policy

Data Retention

While App is Installed

  • Data is retained locally on your device for as long as you use the app
  • You can delete data anytime using "Clear All Data" in Settings

After Uninstallation

  • All data is automatically removed when you uninstall the app
  • No data remains on external servers (because we don't use any)

International Users

Privacy Guardian is available worldwide. Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • No data crosses international borders
  • GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy regulations are inherently respected
  • No cookies or tracking technologies are used

  2. Uninstalling the app
