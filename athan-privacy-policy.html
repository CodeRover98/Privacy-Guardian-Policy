Introduction

Astreon Prayer Times ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Islamic prayer times application.

Our Core Principle: Your location data stays on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or share any of your personal information with third parties or servers.

Information We Collect

Astreon Prayer Times collects minimal information, stored locally on your device only:

Location Data

  • What: GPS coordinates (latitude and longitude)
  • Purpose: Calculate accurate prayer times based on your geographical location
  • Type: Precise location (for accuracy)
  • Storage: Stored locally on your device only
  • Sharing: Never shared with third parties or uploaded to servers
  • Usage: Only when app is active ("While Using App")

App Preferences

  • What: Prayer calculation method, notification settings, audio preferences
  • Purpose: Remember your customizations
  • Storage: Stored locally on your device only
  • Sharing: Never shared

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information exclusively on your device to:

  • Calculate Prayer Times: Use your location to determine accurate prayer times for your area using astronomical calculations
  • Send Notifications: Provide prayer time reminders (Athan) based on your preferences
  • Display Qibla Direction: Show the direction to Mecca from your current location
  • Improve User Experience: Remember your settings (calculation method, Athan sound, notification preferences)

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage Only

  • All data stored locally: Location, preferences, and settings are stored only on your device
  • No cloud storage: We do not upload or store your data on any servers
  • No user accounts: The app does not require creating an account or logging in
  • Protected by Android/iOS: Data is protected by your device's operating system security

No Third-Party Services

  • No analytics or tracking services
  • No advertising networks
  • No data collection services
  • App works entirely offline after initial setup

Information Sharing

We do NOT:

  • Sell your personal information
  • Share your data with advertisers
  • Use tracking or analytics services
  • Store your data on external servers
  • Require user accounts or registration
  • Transmit any data over the internet
  • Access your contacts, photos, or other apps

Your privacy is absolute. All data processing happens locally on your device.

Permissions Required

Android Permissions

  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION: Required to get precise location for accurate prayer time calculations
  • ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION: Required to get approximate location for prayer time calculations
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED: Required to restore prayer time notifications after device restart
  • VIBRATE: Required to provide vibration alerts for prayer notifications

iOS Permissions

  • Location Services (When In Use): Required to calculate prayer times based on your location
  • Notifications: Required to send prayer time reminders (Athan)

Permission Usage Details

Location permission message: "This app needs access to your location to calculate accurate prayer times based on your current position."

  • When used: Only when app is active
  • Frequency: When you open the app or manually refresh prayer times
  • Background usage: No background location tracking
  • Optional: No - required for core functionality

Your Rights and Control

Access Your Data

  • All your data is accessible through the app settings
  • You can view your current location and prayer times anytime
  • No hidden data collection

Delete Your Data

  • Clear app data: Go to device Settings ‚Üí Apps ‚Üí Astreon Prayer Times ‚Üí Clear Data
  • Uninstall: Uninstalling the app automatically deletes all locally stored data
  • No server cleanup needed: Since we don't store data on servers, uninstalling removes everything

Disable Location

  • You can deny location permissions in device settings
  • Note: This will affect prayer time accuracy (app will use last known location)

Disable Notifications

  • Turn off notifications in app settings
  • Or disable in device notification settings

Data NOT Collected

For transparency, here is what we explicitly do NOT collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Financial information
  • Health information
  • Contacts
  • Photos or videos
  • Messages
  • Device identifiers (IMEI, MAC address)
  • Browsing history
  • App usage analytics
  • Crash reports (no crash reporting service)

Children's Privacy

Astreon Prayer Times does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences practicing Islam and does not have age-restricted content.

Parents and guardians should supervise their children's device usage.
